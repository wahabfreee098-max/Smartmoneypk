SmartMoney PK ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website to provide financial education content to readers in Pakistan and elsewhere. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: such as your name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, or any details you submit via a contact form.
  • Automatically collected information: including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site, gathered through standard analytics tools.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: small data files stored on your device, described in more detail below.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and content
  • Send newsletters or updates to subscribers who have opted in
  • Understand how visitors use our site, to improve articles and navigation
  • Display relevant advertising through third-party ad networks
  • Respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form

3. Cookies and tracking technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit. We and our partners (including advertising and analytics providers) may use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our site (analytics)
  • Show advertising that may be more relevant to you

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of our site function.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense and potentially other third-party advertising networks. These providers may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

📌 About Google's advertising cookies

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com. Third-party vendors, including Google, may also use cookies to serve ads based on someone's prior visits to this website.

We do not control the cookies placed by third-party advertising networks. Please refer to each provider's own privacy policy for more information about their data practices.

5. Third-party links

Our articles may contain links to third-party websites, products, or services, including affiliate links to financial apps, banks, or other platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the website (such as email newsletter platforms)
  • Advertising partners, in the form of non-personally-identifiable, aggregated data used for ad targeting
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our legal rights

7. Your rights and choices

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link provided in any email
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalised advertising through your browser or via Google's Ads Settings
  • Contact us to request information about data we may hold about you

8. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out through our Contact page.
